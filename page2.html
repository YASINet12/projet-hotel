Château d'Or is a luxurious hotel located in Rabat, Morocco, offering an exceptional blend of traditional Moroccan architecture and modern amenities. With a rich history of hosting 1,000 visitors annually, the hotel provides a warm, welcoming atmosphere, ideal for both leisure and business travelers. Its prime location allows guests to explore the vibrant culture and history of Rabat while enjoying the comfort of spacious rooms, fine dining, and world-class service. Whether you're visiting for a short stay or an extended retreat, Château d'Or promises an unforgettable experience. Book your stay today and indulge in Moroccan luxury!
